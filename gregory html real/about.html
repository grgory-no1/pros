What we do at Play Forward

At Play Forward, our achievements reflect our commitment to empowering young athletes and strengthening communities.

Our Mission Statement

1.Empowering communities through sport by providing essential equipment to small clubs, ensuring that every athlete has the opportunity to play and thrive.
2.Building a brighter future through sports by equipping small clubs with the tools they need to foster talent, teamwork, and inclusivity for all.
3.Inspiring youth and strengthening communities by delivering quality sports equipment to small clubs, creating pathways for health, development, and social connection.
4.Championing equal access to sports for all by distributing essential equipment to grassroots clubs, nurturing the next generation of athletes and leaders.

Our Workers

At Play Forward, we are dedicated to providing sports equipment to small clubs, treating employees well is essential to fostering a positive and productive work environment. By offering competitive salaries, comprehensive benefits, and opportunities for professional development, the organization demonstrates its commitment to staff wellbeing. Additionally, encouraging a culture of teamwork and inclusivity allows employees to feel valued and connected to the charity's mission. Regular recognition of their contributions, along with flexible working arrangements, further enhances job satisfaction, ensuring that the team is motivated to make a meaningful impact in the communities they serve.
