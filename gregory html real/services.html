How we go about our work?

To qualify for support from Play Forward, the club must meet several key conditions;
1.The club must demonstrate a commitment to inclusivity, actively welcoming participants of all backgrounds and skill levels.
2.The club must have a structured program that promotes regular practice and competition, ensuring that the equipment will be used effectively.
3.The club should also provide evidence of community engagement, showing how it contributes to local development and fosters a positive environment for youth.
4.The club must commit to maintaining the equipment responsibly and providing feedback on its impact, helping the charity measure its effectiveness and refine its support strategies.
