PLAY FORWARD

Welcome

At Play Forward, we believe that every child deserves the opportunity to engage in sports and physical activity. Our mission is to support small sports clubs by providing essential sports equipment, ensuring that financial barriers do not hinder participation. By partnering with local organizations and communities, we aim to foster teamwork, build confidence, and promote healthy lifestyles. Together, we’re creating a brighter future where every young athlete has the tools they need to thrive on and off the field.

Over the past year, we have successfully distributed over 5,000 pieces of sports equipment to more than 150 small sports clubs in the UK, enabling thousands of children to participate in organized sports. Our initiatives have not only increased access to quality gear but also fostered inclusivity, with many clubs reporting higher participation rates among underrepresented groups. Additionally, we’ve seen firsthand the positive impact on youth development, as kids build teamwork skills, boost their confidence, and cultivate a lifelong love for physical activity.
